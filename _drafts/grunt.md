* To enable tab auto-completion for Grunt, add one of the following lines to your `~/.bashrc` or `~/.zshrc` file:
	* `eval "$(grunt --completion=bash)" // Bash`
	* `eval "$(grunt --completion=zsh)" // Zsh`
